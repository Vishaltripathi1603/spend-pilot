# Go-To-Market Strategy: SpendPilot

## 1. The Exact Target User
**Title:** Fractional CTO or "Founding Engineer"
**Company Stage:** Seed to Series A (10–50 employees)
**The Pain:** These users are currently being asked by their CEO or Finance lead to "cut costs" as the initial AI hype cycle cools. They have 20+ seats across Cursor, Claude, and OpenAI and have no central visibility into who is using what.

## 2. Intent & Search Behavior
Right before using SpendPilot, our target user is Googling:
- *"Cursor Business vs Enterprise features"*
- *"Claude Team plan minimum seats"*
- *"How to reduce OpenAI API bill"*
- *"Is GitHub Copilot redundant with Cursor?"*
They are scrolling through the **#engineering** or **#finance** channels in their YC or First Round alumni Slack groups.

## 3. Where They Hang Out
- **Indie Hackers:** Specifically the "Milestones" and "Questions" sections.
- **X (Twitter):** Following accounts like @swyx, @karpathy, and @levelsio where AI stack discussions are frequent.
- **Subreddits:** `r/startups`, `r/aws` (cost optimization threads), and `r/nextjs`.
- **Private Communities:** YC Bookface, Launch House, and specific VC-backed CTO circles.

## 4. The First 100 Users ($0 Budget)
1. **The "Audit Your Stack" Thread on X:** I will post a thread titled *"We analyzed 50 startup AI stacks and found $200k in wastage. Here is the 2-minute tool to check yours."* and tag key AI influencers.
2. **Direct Outreach to Fractional CTOs:** I will cold-DM 20 fractional CTOs on LinkedIn offering the tool as a "free value-add" they can give to their clients to look like heroes.
3. **Product Hunt Launch:** A Tuesday launch scheduled for 12:01 AM PST, focused on the "Free Audit" value prop, not a "Paid Tool" pitch.
4. **Indie Hackers "Show & Tell":** Writing a deep-dive post on "How I built a rule-based engine to audit AI spend" to capture the builder audience.

## 5. The Unfair Distribution Channel
**The Credex Partnership:** As a tool built for the Credex ecosystem, SpendPilot can be featured in the Credex newsletter and on their homepage. This gives us instant access to a high-intent audience already looking for secondary market credits.

## 6. Week-1 Traction Goals
- **Total Visitors:** 1,500
- **Audits Completed:** 600 (40% conversion)
- **High-Savings Leads:** 60 (10% of audits)
- **Consultations Booked:** 6 (10% of high-savings leads)
