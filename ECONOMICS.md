# Unit Economics: SpendPilot

## 1. Lead Value to Credex
**Estimate:** $1,500 per converted lead.
**Reasoning:** The average "High Savings" user is spending >$1,000/mo on APIs. Credex saves them 20% by selling credits. If Credex takes a 10% margin on those credits, that's $100/mo in gross profit. With an LTV (Lifetime Value) of 15 months, a single customer is worth $1,500.

## 2. CAC by Channel
- **Organic Social (X/LinkedIn):** $0.00 (Time investment only).
- **Referral Loop:** $10 (Cost of a small perk/credit per successful referral).
- **Content Marketing:** $50 (Amortized cost of engineering-led content).
**Blended CAC Goal:** < $15.00 per qualified lead.

## 3. The Profitability Math
To be profitable, we need the following funnel:
- **Audit Completed** → **Consultation Booked:** 5%
- **Consultation Booked** → **Credit Purchase:** 20%
**Overall Conversion (Audit → Sale):** 1%
If 100 people run an audit, we get 1 customer worth $1,500. If our cost to get those 100 people is < $1,500 (which it is via organic), the tool is a massive profit driver.

## 4. Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months
To hit $1M ARR, Credex needs ~$83k in monthly gross profit.
**The Requirements:**
1. **Volume:** 830 active customers spending $1,000/mo on credits.
2. **Growth:** At a 1% total conversion rate, we need **83,000 audits per month**.
3. **Viral Coefficient:** We need a K-factor of **> 0.2** (every 5 users bring 1 more) to sustain this volume without paid ads.
4. **Expansion:** We must expand from just "Audit" to "Automated Savings Implementation" (SaaS fee) to increase ARPU.
